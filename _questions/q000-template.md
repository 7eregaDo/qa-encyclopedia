## Intent

> What this question is testing — what the interviewer wants to uncover.

---

## Answer (STAR)

**Situation:** Set the scene. What project, company, or context were you in?

**Task:** What was your specific responsibility or challenge?

**Action:** What did *you* do? Be specific — tools, methods, frameworks used.

**Result:** Quantified outcome. What improved, by how much, what was the business impact?

---

## Seniority Shift

<div class="seniority-shift">

- **Entry:** Focus on understanding the concept and applying it in simple scenarios.
- **Mid:** Focus on tool proficiency and owning the solution end-to-end.
- **Senior:** Focus on strategy, trade-offs, and influencing the team.
- **Lead:** Focus on org-wide impact, stakeholder communication, and policy.

</div>
